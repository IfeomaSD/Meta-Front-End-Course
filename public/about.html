Welcome to Mangata & Gallo luxury jewelry brand! We specialize in creating exquisite pieces for special occasions such as engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. Our brand is mainly online making it easier for our clients to shop from the comfort of their homes. We have a storefront in Austin, Texas where you can browse the atelier. We take pride in our unique designs and high-quality materials that make each piece a true work of art.

Our founder, Mariana, is a trained diamond cutter and metal smith who studied design in school. With her passion for creating beautiful and meaningful jewelry, she has carefully curated a collection that reflects the utmost elegance and sophistication. Mariana's attention to detail is evident in every piece, from the precision of the diamond cuts to the intricate metalwork.

At Mangata & Gallo, we understand that every special occasion is unique, and we strive to make each piece of jewelry as unique as the person wearing it. Our goal is to create timeless pieces that will be treasured for generations to come. We use only the finest materials, including conflict-free diamonds, and precious metals such as platinum, gold, and silver.

Our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship and customer satisfaction is unwavering. We work closely with our clients to ensure that their vision is realized and that their jewelry is of the highest quality. Our team is always available to answer any questions and provide personalized recommendations based on each client's needs.

We believe that every special occasion deserves a piece of jewelry that is as unique and extraordinary as the moment itself. Let us be a part of your special occasion and create a lasting memory that will be cherished forever. Thank you for choosing our luxury jewelry brand.
